Simo Lemhandez

folk

Maker
📌
Hey folks, Simo here, the founder and CEO of folk. We spent the last years rethinking every detail of the ideal CRM and rebuilding every single feature. Turns out, 3k companies were hungry for a new kind of CRM too. It’s even won us multiple awards, like nominated Product of the Year, here on Product Hunt. We’ve been working hard on making folk a major improvement from the traditional CRM. It’s simple, customizable, and integrates into your sales world. And I’m very proud of the user feedback we’ve gotten so far. But all of this still feel like a major evolution, not yet a revolution. Even with a better experience, the paradigm still rest on the same principles: someone required to spend most of their time in front of their screen. Updating a contact. Finding who to reach out to. Sending an email. We want folk to do the busy work for you, so you can focus on growing your service business. We want folk to be like the sales assistant your team never had. We want to give you your time back, so you can spend it building real relationships with your clients. We’re fortunate to have all the underlying infrastructure. Your contacts, companies, the metadata associated, enriched, connected to your emails, calendars, social media and more. Our plan is to absorb this complexity for you and suggest the next, best action. Today is a first step toward that vision, with the launch of AI Follow-ups. We identify the best leads you should follow-up with based on your past conversations, using AI. We also suggest the reply, in your tone. Sign up and give it try!
